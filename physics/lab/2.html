Experiment 2: Half Wave Rectifier

Objectives

  1. To construct a half wave rectifier
  2. To observe input/output waveforms with and without filter capacitors using CRO
  3. To measure voltages and calculate ripple factor
  4. To analyze ripple factor variation with different filter capacitor values at fixed load resistance

Components Required

  • Transformer (6V-0-6V)
  • Diode 1N4007
  • Filter Capacitors:
    • 4.7μF
    • 10μF
    • 47μF
    • 100μF
  • Decade Resistance Box
  • AC & DC Voltmeters
  • Digital Multimeter
  • CRO (Cathode Ray Oscilloscope)
