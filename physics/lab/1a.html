Experiment 1A: V-I Characteristics of Diodes

Aim

  1. To Study Forward Characteristics of Ge and Si Diodes
  2. To Study Reverse Characteristics of Ge Diode

Apparatus Required

  • Battery
  • Rheostat
  • Voltmeter
  • Milli ammeter
  • Micro ammeter
  • Resistance Box
  • Keys
  • Diode IN 4007 (Si)
  • Diode OA 79 (Ge)

Circuit Diagram

Principle

The diode equation governing the behavior is:

\[ I = I_0 \left(e^{\frac{qV}{nkT}} - 1\right) = I_0 \left(e^{\frac{V}{nV_T}} - 1\right) \]

Where:

  • \( I_0 \) = Reverse Saturation Current
  • \( n \) = Ideality Factor
  • \( V_T = \frac{kT}{q} = 26 \\text{ mV} \)
  • \( n = \frac{q}{kT} \times \\text{Slope} = \frac{1}{V_T} \times \\text{Slope} \)
