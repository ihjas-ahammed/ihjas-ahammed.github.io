Experiment 1A: V-I Characteristics of Diodes

Aim

  1. To Study Forward Characteristics of Ge and Si Diodes
  2. To Study Reverse Characteristics of Ge Diode

Principle

The diode equation governing the behavior is:

I = I₀(e^(qV/nkT) - 1) = I₀(e^(V/nVT) - 1)

Where:

  • I₀ = Reverse Saturation Current
  • n = Ideality Factor
  • VT = (kT/q) = 26 mV
  • n = (q/kT) × Slope = (1/VT) × Slope
