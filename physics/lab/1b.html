Experiment 1B: Photodiode Characteristics

Objectives

  1. To study I-V characteristics of photodiode
  2. To determine the power responsivity by analyzing photocurrent variations with respect to:
    • Reverse voltage
    • Light intensity

Circuit Diagram

        graph LR
            A[Battery] --> B[Rheostat and Resistor]
            B --> C[Ammeter]
            C --> E[Photodiode]
            B --> D[Voltmeter]
            D -->|parallel| E
            E --> A

Experimental Procedure

  1. Set up the circuit as shown in the diagram above
  2. For I-V Characteristics:
    • Vary voltage from 0.1V to 2.5V in steps
    • Record corresponding current readings
    • Repeat for different LED power levels (10mW, 21mW)
  3. For Power Responsivity:
    • Maintain constant voltage (-2V)
    • Vary LED power from 10mW to 30mW
    • Record photocurrent readings

Calculations

Power Responsivity = ΔIPD / ΔPLED

Where:

  • ΔIPD = Change in photodiode current (A)
  • ΔPLED = Change in LED power (W)
